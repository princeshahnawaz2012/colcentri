2. Start working on Kinubi

The collaboration and communication Kinubi software works with Projects.

ï¿½ What is a Project?
In the platform, the word Project is used to refer to any topic where there is communication and exchange of files between more than one person.
A professor can create a Project for his or her math class and invite all the students to join it. He or she can upload the syllabus, lecture notes, open conversations, send them notifications.
For a lawyer, a Project can be a costumer of his or her law office where he or she needs to deliver files about a case, or asking for information for a trial.
For a advertising agency, a Project can be the marketing campaign of a costumer. There the files and requests can be uploaded.
As you can see, a Project can refer to a variety of topics, sectors and different areas.
To make it easy for the user the clasification of the projects that can be related, we have created Group of Projects. Group of Projects is a grouping label of projects, which allows good arrangement of projects within the table of projects.

Briefly, the steps to start working on projects is as follows Start working on projects

  1. Create a Group of Projects
  2. Create a Project and assign it to a Group of Projects
  3. Invite users to participate in the Project
  4. Start working: Create conversations, Create pages, Upload files, See calendar with tasks, Create tasks...

Step 1. Create a Group of Projects
Above
Click on Collaborative environment/ Projects.
In this section you can see a summary table of all the projects in which you participate.
The owner of the Project is the person who created it.
The table also includes the role that your user has in the project, in other words, the degrees of permission you have to work in a given project. Later the existing diverse roles in a project will be explained.
Click on the button Create Group
Here you can name the Group of Projects you want to create.
Click on the button Save.
Step 2. Create a Project and assign it to a Group of Projects
Above
Click on button Create Project
Here you can name the Project you want to create.
Assign the Project to one the Group of Projects you created.
Click on button Save.
Step 3. Invite users to participate in the Project
Above
Click again on the section Collaborative environment/ Projects
Click on the Project you want to work on.
Click on the People team icon from the project's functionalities menu.

Enter the e-mails or usernames of the people you want to add to the project.
If there are many users, you should include their e-mails or usernames separated by commas.
If the person is already registered on the platform, he or she will be automatically added to the Project.
If the person is not registered in the platform, he or she will recieve an invitation to register on Kinubi to the e-mail you provided.

After you have included the username you should select the role you want this person have in the project.
Finally, you have to click on button Invite.

Note: When the users you invite have diverse roles, you have to repeat these roles for each role.
User's Roles in a Project

You can invite people to a Project with diverse degrees of permission. A summary of the diverse roles you can use are presented to you.
  • Chief of the Project: He or she has full access to the management of a Project, this person has all the permissions that are possible.
  • Participant: He or she has full access to work in a Project but invite users or change the settings of the Project.
  • Commentatora: He or she has participation access to all elements that have been created in the Project.
  • Observer: He or she can see the contents of the Project.
Paso 4. Start working
Arriba
Now, you can start working in the Project. You only need to go to Collaborative environment/ Projects, click on the Project, and start exploring the functionalities of the project you need, through the menu of icons.
