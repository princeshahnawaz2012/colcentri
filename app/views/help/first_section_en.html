1. Structure of the working environment of Kinubi
This is the start up screen that appears when Kinubi is started. The sections of the platform are explained to help you understand it quickly.
The platform has a main central part and a right side part.
On the central part you can use all the tools that Kinubi offer you.
The right side part is split in two parts, the personal environment and the collaborative environment.
The personal environment contains only data related to my username.
The collaborative environment includes all the functions related to the projects where I am part.
In the following section an overview of the personal and collaborative environments is described.
1.1. Personal environment
Arriba
  • Recent activity: On recent activity you will be able to see all activities related to your username.
  • Calendar: here you will be able to see the tasks that are of your interest. You can create personal inputs.
  • Tasks: Here you can see all the projects where you participate.
  • Files: Here you have a private space for your personal files so you can have access to them in cloud.
  • User settings:
    • User's information: Here you have your personal contact information.
    • Profile image: Here you can save the image that will be displayed next to the actions you do.
    • Notifications: Here you have options to choose from the actions performed in the projects and select which if them you want to be sent to your e-mail address.
    • User's Panel: Here you will be able to purchase services provided by Kinubi and the details of your purchase.
    • Invoicing: Here you have a table containing your invoicing history of your purchases.
1.2. Collaborative environment
Arriba
  • Recent activity: Recent information about the project in which you participate.
  • Calendar: Here the tasks of all the projects where you are part of will be shown.
  • Projects: A table containing all the projects in which you are participating will be shown.
1.3. Project's functionalities menu
Arriba
Once you have entered on the section Collaborative environment/ Projects, you will be able to acces the project on which you want to start working.
Here an overview of the functionalities you will come across are presented in the form of icons, in working area of projects next to the Create Group and Creat Project buttons.
  • Recent activity: Here you will be able to see latest news of what is happening in the projects.
  • Calendar: Here you will be able to see the tasks with assigned responsible in the project.
  • Conversations: Here you can create conversations of a project.
  • Pages: Here you can create contents in the form of wiki.
  • Tasks: Here you can create tasks, assign responsible and participants, set the start and end dates.
  • Files: Here you can see the history of all the files which have been uploaded to this project.
  • People team: Here you can invited users to participate in a project when you have become the chief of the project, or see all the people who participate in the project.
  • Settings: As the chief of the project you will be able to manage the basic information of the project.
